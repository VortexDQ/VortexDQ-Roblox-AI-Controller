📦 Installation Guide

  1. Copy these files to your Roblox Plugins folder:
    • plugin/Main.lua
    • plugin/WebSocketClient.lua
    • plugin/Controller.lua
    • plugin/InstanceManager.lua
  2. Find your Roblox plugins folder:
    • Windows: %APPDATA%\Roblox\Plugins\
    • Mac: ~/Library/Application Support/Roblox/Plugins/
  3. Restart Roblox Studio
  4. Open any place and click Plugins → VortexDQ AI
